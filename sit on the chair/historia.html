Tech Tweaks to Get More Info From Medical Devices

Medical devices such as insulin pumps and continuous glucose monitors are being used by many of the 30,000 Americans diagnosed each year with Type 1 diabetes to manage the condition. A group of tech-savvy patients and parents of diabetic children wanted more access to their data and figured out a way to essentially hack a glucose monitor made by Dexcom to stream blood-sugar data to the Internet so it can be viewed remotely in real-time
